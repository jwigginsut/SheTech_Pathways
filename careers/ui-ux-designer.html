Women Who Lead the Way

Julie Zhuo

Co-founder of Sundial, former VP of Product Design at Facebook


Photo Credit: Washington Speakers Bureau

“Design is about making things work better for people.”

Julie Zhuo is a Chinese-American product design leader, entrepreneur, and author known for shaping user experience in large-scale digital products. A former Vice President of Product Design at Facebook, she now co-founds and leads the data-intelligence company Sundial. Zhuo’s work bridges design, technology, and leadership, influencing how digital teams build products for billions of users.

Mini-Activity: Try This!

Design Your Own App Screen

  1. Choose an app you use every day.
  2. Redesign one screen on paper or in a free tool like Figma.
  3. Ask a friend to test it—can they figure it out without help?

That’s UX in action!

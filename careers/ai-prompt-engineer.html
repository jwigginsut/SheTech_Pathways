Mini-Activity: Try This!

Design Your First Prompt
Ask an AI to act as a career coach for high school students interested in STEM.

  1. Write one short prompt
  2. Then rewrite it to be clearer, more creative, or more specific
  3. Notice how small changes completely change the results—this is prompt engineering in action.

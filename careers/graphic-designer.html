Mini-Activity: Try This!

Design Your Personal Logo

  1. Choose a word that represents you (bold, curious, creative).
  2. Sketch a logo using shapes or letters
  3. Recreate it digitally using Canva or Figma.
  4. Try different colors and fonts to see how design changes the message.

Women Who Lead the Way

Tracy Chou

Founder & CEO, Block Party

Photo Credit: Executive Women Media

“I love building things that help people—and working to make tech a place where everyone belongs.”

Tracy Chou is an American software engineer, entrepreneur, and advocate for diversity and inclusion in the technology industry. She is best known as the founder and CEO of Block Party, a company developing consumer tools to combat online harassment and improve digital privacy. Her activism has made her a leading voice in promoting transparency and equity in tech workplaces.

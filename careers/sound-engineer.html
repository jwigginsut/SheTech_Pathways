Women Who Lead the Way

Dr. Susan Rogers

Professor, Berklee College of Music

Photo Credit: Berklee College of Music

“Sound is emotional. When you understand it, you can move people in powerful ways.”

Dr. Susan Rogers is best known for her groundbreaking work as Prince’s staff engineer, helping shape iconic albums like Purple Rain. Today, she combines deep technical expertise with neuroscience research to advance how we understand music and sound perception.

Mini-Activity: Try This!

Transform the Mood
Use a free app like GarageBand or BandLab to record a short sound clip (your voice, clapping, or an instrument). Experiment with volume levels, reverb, and EQ to change how it feels. Notice how small technical changes can completely transform the mood.

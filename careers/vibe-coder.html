Women Who Lead the Way

Allison Parrish

Assistant Arts Professor at NYU’s Interactive Telecommunications Program

Photo Credit: NYU TISCH

“Code is a creative medium—just like language or music.”

Allison Parrish is a pioneer in creative coding and AI-generated art, blending poetry, machine learning, and programming to explore how humans and computers create together. Her work inspires a new generation of coders to see technology as expressive, playful, and deeply human. She is originally from West Bountiful, Utah and currently lives in Brooklyn, NY.

Mini-Activity: Try This!

Vibe Coding Challenge

  1. Open p5.js Web Editor or Replit
  2. Create a simple interactive visual (shapes that move with your mouse or music)
  3. Add your own “vibe”: colors, motion, or text
  4. Bonus: Ask an AI tool to help you remix or enhance your code

✨ Congrats—you just vibe coded.

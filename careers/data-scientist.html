Mini-Activity: Try This!

Be a Data Detective:
Find a public dataset (like music trends or sports stats). Ask a question such as “What makes a song popular?” Then:

  1. Sort the data in a spreadsheet
  2. Create a simple chart
  3. Share one insight you discovered

That’s data science in action!

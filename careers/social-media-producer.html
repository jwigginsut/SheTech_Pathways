Mini-Activity: Try This!

Create a 30-Second Trend Video

  1. Pick a trending topic you care about (fashion, sports, STEM, gaming).
  2. Film a short vertical video explaining it in your own words.
  3. Add captions, music, and a hook in the first 3 seconds.
  4. Share it privately or with friends and track engagement.

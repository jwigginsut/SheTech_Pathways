Women Who Lead the Way

Joy Buolamwini

Founder:  Algorithmic Justice League

Photo credit: Algorithmic Justice League / MIT Media Lab / Wikimedia Commons

“AI can be a mirror that reflects our values—or a magnifier that amplifies our biases.”

Joy Buolamwini is a computer scientist, artist, and AI ethics researcher known for exposing racial and gender bias in facial recognition systems. She founded the Algorithmic Justice League to advocate for equitable and accountable artificial intelligence. Her work, bridging art and science, has shaped global conversations on algorithmic fairness and human rights in technology.

Women Who Lead the Way

President & Chief Operating Officer, SpaceX

Photo credit: National Women’s History Museum

“I love working on things that have never been done before—engineering makes that possible.”

Gwynne Shotwell is a trained mechanical engineer who has played a pivotal role in advancing reusable rocket technology and commercial spaceflight. Her leadership and engineering expertise have helped transform SpaceX into a global leader in aerospace innovation, opening new possibilities for space exploration and technology-driven careers.

Mini-Activity: Try This!

Build a Simple Machine Challenge

  1. Design a small device at home that can lift a book using everyday materials (cardboard, string, pencils).
  2. Test different designs and see which one lifts the most weight—just like a mechanical engineer experimenting with prototypes.

Mini-Activity: Try This!

Tech Fix Challenge

  1. Ask a friend or family member about a tech problem they’ve had (slow computer, Wi-Fi issues, forgotten password).
  2. Research the issue
  3. Write step-by-step instructions to fix it
  4. Test your solution and explain it clearly—just like an IT Support Specialist would!

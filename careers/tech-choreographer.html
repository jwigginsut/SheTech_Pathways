Claire Bardainne is a French visual and digital artist, stage designer, and choreographer recognized for blending technology, scenography, and performance. Born in 1978 in Grenoble, she co-directs the company Adrien M & Claire B with Adrien Mondot, creating immersive works that merge visual art and live performance through poetic use of interactive media.

Women Who Lead the Way

Jeanne Gang

Founder:  Studio Gang


Photo Credit: Studio Gang / Wikimedia Commons

“Architecture is about bringing people together and imagining a better future through design.”

Jeanne Gang is an American architect and the founding partner of Studio Gang, an international architecture and urban design practice headquartered in Chicago with offices in New York, San Francisco, and Paris. She is recognized for blending sculptural form, structural innovation, and social and ecological awareness to create architecture that connects people, communities, and the environment.
